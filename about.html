About Me

Salman Chohan

Behind the Lens

Hello there, I'm Salman, the person behind SalCaptures – a Manchester-based photographer specialising in real estate, wedding, and architectural photography. I'm passionate about creating images that feel real, personal, and timeless.

My journey into photography started with a love for exploring the city – climbing rooftops, chasing sunrises, and capturing Manchester's unique energy. That passion has grown into a full-time creative career serving clients across the North West.

Whether you're selling a property, celebrating your big day, or showcasing a building, I combine technical skill with an eye for detail to bring out the best. My goal is simple: high-quality, affordable photography tailored just for you.

When I'm not behind the lens, you'll find me exploring Manchester, brewing coffee, or working on cars. Let's create something unforgettable together.

Serving Manchester, Cheshire, and the North West – get in touch for a free quote.
