About

Hello there, I'm Salman, the person behind SalCaptures – a Manchester-based photographer specialising in real estate, wedding, and architectural photography. I'm passionate about creating images that feel real, personal, and timeless. Whether you're selling a property, celebrating your big day, or showcasing a building, I combine technical skill with an eye for detail to bring out the best.

My goal is to offer high-quality, affordable photography tailored just for you. When I'm not behind the lens, you'll find me exploring Manchester, brewing coffee, or working on cars. Let's create something unforgettable together.

Serving all of Manchester, Cheshire, and the North West – get in touch for a free quote.
